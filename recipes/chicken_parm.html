Chicken Parm

Delicious fried chicken cutlets topped with melted mozzarella cheese

Ingredients

  • Chicken cutlet
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan
  2. Dip cutlets in flour then egg and finally bread crumbs
  3. Fry to 165 F
  4. Top with sauce and cheese
  5. Broil until cheese melted
